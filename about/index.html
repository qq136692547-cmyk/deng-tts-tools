About

Why this site exists

TikTok Shop is the fastest-growing marketplace of 2024–2026. Most new sellers only know the 6% referral commission TikTok shows in the dashboard. The real fee stack includes the Payment processing fee, payment processing, cross-border fees, and creator commission — together typically 12%–15%, sometimes 22%+ with creators.

DengTTSTools makes the real fee stack visible. Calculator for the total TikTok fees. Profit calculator that includes creator commission and ad spend. Side-by-side comparison vs Amazon FBA on the same product — so you can decide which platform actually pays you more for the SKU you have in mind.
