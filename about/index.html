About

Why this site exists

TikTok Shop is the fastest-growing marketplace of 2024–2026. Most new sellers only know the 6% referral commission TikTok shows in the dashboard. The real fee stack includes FBT fulfillment, cross-border fees, and creator commission — together typically 12%–15%, sometimes 22%+ with creators.

TTCalc makes the real fee stack visible. Calculator for the total TikTok fees. Profit calculator that includes creator commission and ad spend. Side-by-side comparison vs Amazon FBA on the same product — so you can decide which platform actually pays you more for the SKU you have in mind.

Author: L.D. Studio

Independent developer and TikTok Shop seller. Built TTCalc in 2025 after losing money on a product that looked profitable on the Seller Center dashboard but wasn't once the full fee stack was accounted for. The goal: make the real numbers visible to every seller, for free.

Expertise: TikTok Shop fee structure, FBT fulfillment economics, cross-platform profitability comparison (TikTok vs Amazon FBA), creator commission modeling, and return cost analysis.
