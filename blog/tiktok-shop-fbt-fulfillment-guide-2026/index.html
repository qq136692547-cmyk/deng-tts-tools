TikTok Shop FBT Fulfillment Costs 2026: Complete Fee Breakdown

If you sell on TikTok Shop in the US, you no longer have a choice about using TikTok's fulfillment services. Since March 31, 2026, independent shipping is gone — every seller must use one of three approved logistics pathways: Fulfilled by TikTok (FBT), Upgraded TikTok Shipping, or Collections by TikTok (CBT). FBT is the most popular option because it's the closest equivalent to Amazon FBA.

Here's what FBT actually costs per unit, how multi-item discounts change the math, what storage fees look like, and when you're better off with one of the other options.

What Is Fulfilled by TikTok?

Fulfilled by TikTok (FBT) is TikTok Shop's in-house fulfillment service. You send your inventory to TikTok's partner warehouses, and they handle storage, picking, packing, and last-mile delivery to customers. It mirrors Amazon FBA's structure, though the network is smaller and less geographically distributed (source: Dashboardly, Apr 2026).

The key advantage: FBT exempts you from late-delivery and out-of-stock performance penalties. The trade-off is that you lose control over shipping speed and carrier selection.

FBT Per-Unit Fees (2026)

FBT charges a per-unit fee for each item shipped. The base rate for a single-item order is $3.58 (source: hyperfocus.tech, 2026). This covers picking, packing, and shipping within TikTok's delivery network.

The official TikTok Seller Center rate card confirms that single-unit fulfillment fees start at $3.58 per item (source: TikTok Seller Center). Heavy items (4+ lbs) cost more, though exact rates vary by negotiated terms.

Some sources cite starting rates of $4.28 per unit depending on weight tier and product category (source: Printify, Jun 2026). For the examples in this guide, we'll use the $3.58 rate which covers most standard-weight products under 4 lbs.

Multi-Item Discounts

Effective January 12, 2026, TikTok reduced multi-unit fulfillment fees by up to 24% for orders with 2, 3, and 4+ items in the 0-4 lbs weight tiers (source: TikTok Seller Center FBT Rate Card).

Storage Fees

FBT offers 60 days of free storage for new inventory. After the free period, monthly storage fees apply at rates of approximately $0.75 to $1.50 per cubic foot depending on product size and turnover (source: Darkroom Agency, Jun 2026).

Compared to Amazon FBA's storage fees ($0.78/cu ft standard, $2.40/cu ft Q4), TikTok's storage is competitive for fast-moving inventory. The 60-day free window is a meaningful advantage if you can maintain a sell-through rate of 30+ days.

Storage fees are charged monthly based on the average daily volume of cubic feet your inventory occupies. There's no aged-inventory surcharge (unlike Amazon's 181-day fee), which makes FBT more forgiving for slower-moving products.

Other Fulfillment Options

FBT isn't your only option. TikTok mandates three fulfillment pathways:

OptionHow It WorksCostBest For
FBT TikTok stores and ships $2.86–$3.58/unit + storage Sellers who want hands-off fulfillment
Upgraded TikTok Shipping You pack, TikTok provides discounted labels ~20% below carrier retail rates Sellers with in-house packing capacity
Collections by TikTok (CBT) TikTok picks up from your warehouse ~30% below Upgraded rates Sellers with their own warehouse and volume

For most new sellers, FBT is the simplest option. Upgraded TikTok Shipping and CBT require more operational setup but can save 20-30% on shipping costs if you already have packing infrastructure (source: Dashboardly).

When FBT Makes Sense

Choose FBT when:

  • You're a new seller without packing and shipping infrastructure
  • Your products are lightweight (under 4 lbs)
  • You can maintain inventory turnover within 60 days (to avoid storage fees)
  • You want to avoid late-delivery and out-of-stock performance penalties
  • You're selling products that encourage multi-item orders (bundles, sets)

Choose another option when:

  • Your products are heavy (4+ lbs) — FBT's per-unit cost rises significantly
  • You already have a warehouse and negotiated carrier rates
  • You ship high volumes (CBT's 30% savings add up quickly)
  • Your products are oversized and FBT's storage rates eat into margins

Quick Summary

FBT is mandatory for US TikTok Shop sellers, but it's not a one-size-fits-all service. The math changes significantly depending on your item weight, order size, and storage turnover. A few key takeaways:

  • Single-unit FBT costs $3.58, but multi-item orders drop to $2.86/unit — bundle strategically.
  • 60-day free storage is a real advantage over Amazon FBA, but only if you sell through within that window.
  • For heavy or oversized products, Upgraded TikTok Shipping or CBT can save 20-30%.
  • FBT has fewer surcharges than FBA — no aged-inventory, low-inventory, or inbound placement fees.
