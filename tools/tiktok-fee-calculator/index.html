How the TikTok Shop Fee Calculator Works

The TikTok Shop fee calculator takes your sale price and product category as inputs, applies TikTok's current fee structure, and outputs exactly what you'll receive per order. The tool sums the base commission (8% for most categories), the Payment processing fee (2%), and payment processing (1.8%) into one transparent fee stack. If you use creator marketing or ship cross-border, it factors in those optional costs too. The result shows your TikTok payout alongside the percentage TikTok takes from your sale — so you can stop guessing whether the 6% referral fee is the only fee you owe.

Fee Breakdown

Many sellers are shocked when their first settlement statement arrives because TikTok's 6% referral fee isn't the only deduction. The platform stacks three mandatory fees on top of each other, plus optional ones depending on how you sell. For a $29.99 item, the combined fee typically lands between 11% and 12% — not 8%. The table below lists every fee this calculator tracks, along with its rate and when it applies. Understanding how these charges stack is the key to pricing accurately and avoiding margin surprises.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your product's sale price (including shipping) in the Sale price field.
  2. Select your category from the dropdown — the commission rate updates automatically.
  3. If you sell through creator affiliates, set the Creator commission percentage; leave at 0 if not.
  4. Toggle Cross-border order to Yes if you sell outside the US.
  5. Enter the shipping charged to buyer, or set 0 if shipping is free.
  6. Adjust the return rate to see how refunds affect your net payout.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the calculator show different fees than my TikTok seller dashboard?

TikTok's seller dashboard often shows the settlement total after commission is deducted, but may bundle and payment processing into a single line. This calculator separates them so you can see exactly what each fee costs. Small discrepancies (1–2%) can also come from cross-border fees or temporary commission adjustments during promotions.

Is 8% the only fee I pay?

No. The 6% is the referral fee for most categories, but TikTok also charges a 2% Payment processing fee and 1.8% payment processing. These three stack together, bringing the real take rate to roughly 11.8% for most sellers — not 8%.

What is the Payment processing fee?

(Advertising Fund Fee) is a 1.8% fee TikTok charges to invest in platform-wide marketing and traffic acquisition. It applies to all orders, is not optional, and is charged separately from the base commission.

How often do TikTok fee rates change?

TikTok typically adjusts its fee structure once or twice a year, sometimes with category-specific promotions. This calculator reflects 2026 rates, but we recommend verifying after any major policy announcement or when entering a new category.

Does the calculator include return costs?

Yes. Enter your return rate and the calculator estimates both the refund loss and the return handling fee, then shows your net after returns. This gives you a realistic picture of what each shipped order actually earns after customer returns.
