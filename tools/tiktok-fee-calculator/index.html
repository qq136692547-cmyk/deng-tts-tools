What the TikTok Shop fees actually are

01 / explainer
[ 01 ]

Base commission (6%)

6% flat for all US categories. Payment processing is included in this rate. No separate processing fee. Charged on total sale price including shipping.

[ 02 ]

FBT fulfillment

$2.86/$3.20/$3.58 per unit depending on size. Mandatory since March 31, 2026. Fulfilled by TikTok (FBT) — you ship bulk to TikTok's warehouse, they handle last-mile.

[ 03 ]

Creator commission

1%–30% (you set it). Paid to TikTok creators/affiliates who promote your product. Stacks ON TOP of all fees above. Set to 0 if you don't use creators.

[ 04 ]

What's NOT included

This calculator doesn't include: TikTok ad spend, aged-inventory surcharges, or removal/disposal fees. Those vary widely by seller.

FAQ

02 / questions
Is the 6% referral fee all TikTok takes?

The 6% referral fee includes payment processing. On top of that you may have FBT fulfillment ($2.86–$3.58/unit), creator commission (0–30%), cross-border fees (1%), and return costs. The real total typically ranges from 10% to 22%+ depending on your setup.

Do I pay creator commission on top of everything?

Yes. Creator commission is set by you (0–30%) and stacks on top of the 6% referral fee and FBT fulfillment. A product that's profitable at 0% creator commission can flip negative at 25%.

How do TikTok Shop fees compare to Amazon FBA?

On headline commission: TikTok 6% vs Amazon 15% — TikTok wins. On total fee stack including all fees: TikTok 6% + FBT vs Amazon ~35% (with placement, low-inventory, fulfillment) — TikTok still wins by a large margin. But Amazon includes Prime shipping and warehouse fulfillment in that fee. Use the side-by-side calculator for a true comparison.
