What the 5 TikTok Shop fees actually are

01 / explainer
[ 01 ]

Base commission

5%–10% depending on category. The headline number TikTok shows. Electronics 5%, Fashion 10%, most others 8%. Charged on total sale price including shipping.

[ 03 ]

Payment processing

~1.8% paid to the payment processor (Stripe/Adyen). Min $0.30 per order. Shown as a separate line, not a commission.

[ 04 ]

Cross-border fee

~1% on international orders. Applies when you sell to buyers outside your seller region (e.g. US-based seller shipping to a UK buyer).

[ 05 ]

Creator commission

1%–30% (you set it). Paid to TikTok creators/affiliates who promote your product. Stacks ON TOP of all four fees above. Set to 0 if you don't use creators.

[ 06 ]

What's NOT included

This calculator doesn't include: TikTok ad spend, return processing, fulfillment shipping, removal/disposal fees, or aged-inventory surcharges. Those vary widely by seller.

FAQ

02 / questions
Is the 8% commission all TikTok takes?

Application Fund Fee — a 2% platform fee that TikTok charges on every transaction, separate from the base commission. It's listed as a "platform fee" on your statement, not a commission, which is why many sellers overlook it.

How do TikTok Shop fees compare to Amazon FBA?

On headline commission: TikTok 8% vs Amazon 15% — TikTok wins. On total fee stack including all fees: TikTok ~12% vs Amazon ~35% (with placement, low-inventory, fulfillment) — TikTok still wins by a large margin. But Amazon includes Prime shipping and warehouse fulfillment in that fee. Use the side-by-side calculator for a true comparison.
