When TikTok wins, when Amazon wins

01 / decision guide
[ TTS ]

TikTok wins when

You sell a trending / impulse / low-COGS product with strong visual appeal. Video converts well, so organic reach can replace ad spend. Fulfillment costs are low because you ship in bulk to TikTok's warehouse.

[ FBA ]

Amazon wins when

You sell a high-COGS product where organic search (instead of paid ads) drives most sales. Prime shipping gives you a Buy Box boost. Long-tail search terms matter more than impulse appeal.

[ 50/50 ]

Run both

Most successful multi-channel sellers run Amazon for the long-tail organic demand and TikTok Shop for the trend-driven impulse buys. The same SKU, two audiences, two different profit profiles.
