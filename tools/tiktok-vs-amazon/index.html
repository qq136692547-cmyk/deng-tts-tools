How the TikTok Shop vs Amazon FBA Calculator Works

This calculator takes the same product and runs it through both TikTok Shop and Amazon FBA fee structures simultaneously. You enter your sale price, product cost, and shipping costs for each platform. The tool applies TikTok's fee stack (6% referral + 1.8% payment) on one side and Amazon's fee stack (referral fee + FBA fulfillment + storage + placement) on the other. It then shows net profit, margin, and ROI for both platforms side by side, plus a winner declaration so you know exactly where to list.

Fee Comparison Breakdown

TikTok Shop and Amazon FBA charge fees in fundamentally different ways. TikTok uses a simple percentage-based stack totaling roughly 11.8%, while Amazon layers category-dependent referral fees, size-tier-based fulfillment fees, and storage fees that can reach 30% or more. The table below compares each fee component side by side. For most standard-size items under $30, TikTok is cheaper — but Amazon's fees can drop below TikTok's on high-ticket electronics where the referral rate is only 8%.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which platform is cheaper, TikTok Shop or Amazon FBA?

For most standard-size items under $50, TikTok Shop is cheaper because its fee stack totals roughly 11.8% with no per-unit fulfillment charge. Amazon FBA adds fulfillment fees ($3–$5 per unit) on top of referral fees, pushing total costs to 25–35%. However, on high-ticket electronics, Amazon's 8% referral can make it competitive.

At what price point does Amazon become cheaper?

Amazon rarely beats TikTok on fee percentage alone, but the gap narrows on electronics over $100 where Amazon's referral drops to 8%. The real deciding factor is fulfillment cost — if your item is small and light, FBA fulfillment is cheap; if it's bulky, FBA fees can erase your margin entirely.

Does the Amazon side include Prime benefits?

The calculator focuses on fees and profit, not Prime eligibility. FBA automatically includes Prime, which typically boosts conversion rates 20–30%. TikTok Shop doesn't have a Prime equivalent, but its discovery-driven traffic can offset this. Factor conversion differences into your decision separately.

Does the TikTok side include shipping costs?

Yes. You enter your TikTok shipping cost separately from Amazon's inbound shipping. TikTok sellers who fulfill their own orders typically pay $3–$5 per shipment, while FBA sellers pay inbound freight to Amazon's fulfillment centers. Both are factored into the net profit comparison.
